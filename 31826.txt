What is imposter syndrome - Maryam
- Have you ever felt you're not good enough or that you don't belong despite your achievements?

That feeling is when you doubt your abilities and a persistent fear of being exposed as a fraud, even when there's evidence to prove your competence

Change mindset to be a positive thing

Common signs and experiences
1. Discounting accomplishments
2. Fear of failture
3. Overworking and perfectionism 
4. Comparing yourself to others
5. Difficulty accepting praise 

We all will face failure

How do you write a competitive resume when you lack work experience?
Moderator
15 minutes ago
Focus on showcasing your skills and impact through projects, coursework, and activities, highlight what you built, the tools you used, and the results (even small ones), include relevant classes, leadership, or volunteer work, and tailor your resume to the role so it clearly shows your potential even without formal work experience.

How to emphasize soft skills as well as technical skills?
Moderator
12 minutes ago
Show soft skills through your experiences, not just by listing them. Describe what you did, how you collaborated or communicated, and the impact (e.g., teamwork, leadership, problem-solving) alongside the technical tools you used.
